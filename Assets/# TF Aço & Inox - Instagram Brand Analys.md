3. **Primary CTA**: Vibrant Orange (`#FF6B00`) - stands out dramatically
4. **Secondary CTA**: Transparent with white border
5. **Accents**: Gold (`#FFB800`) for trust badges/quality indicators

## Photography Style

### Composition Characteristics
1. **High Contrast Lighting**
   - Dramatic industrial lighting
   - Deep shadows with bright highlights
   - Welding sparks and metal reflections

2. **Scale & Perspective**
   - Wide-angle shots of large steel structures
   - Aerial/elevated perspectives showing project scale
   - Close-ups of precision welding and craftsmanship

3. **Subject Matter**
   - Heavy-duty steel structures
   - Industrial manufacturing processes
   - Corporate credibility shots (awards, team portraits)
   - Behind-the-scenes construction work

### Image Treatment
- **Saturation**: Moderate to high for industrial materials
- **Vignetting**: Subtle darkening at edges for focus
- **Sharpness**: Very high to showcase precision
- **Grain**: Minimal, maintaining professional quality

## Typography Preferences

### Observed Patterns
- **Font Family**: Bold, clean sans-serif fonts
- **Weight**: Heavy/Bold weights for headlines
- **Legibility**: High priority - text overlays always readable against complex backgrounds
- **Case**: Uppercase for emphasis, mixed case for body text
- **Text Shadows**: Used frequently on light text over dark/busy backgrounds
